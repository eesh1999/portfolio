01 / The Premise

When physics breaks, Design begins.

This project is a thought experiment: What happens to "User Experience" when the fundamental laws of nature are inverted? I simulated a civilization dealing with three constraints:

  • 1. Reverse Gravity: Objects fall to the sky. Ceilings are floors.
  • 2. Infrared Vision: Humans perceive heat, not light. Color theory is obsolete.
  • 3. Solid Water: H2O is rock-hard at room temp. It must be "manufactured."
